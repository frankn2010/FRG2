About Forge Reality Games

Forge Reality Games is a small (very small), independent game studio with the goal of creating games without many of today's modern business practices (you know what I'm talking about). It is our mission to never ask you for money to buy the color blue or any color for that matter. We want our games to be fun without any kind of microtransactions distracting you from that fun. Our first game, Jump!, will be launching soon on itch.io, and if it does well enough we will look into a possible Steam launch. Keep your eyes and ears out for more information soon. And please consider following us on social media. We need all the help we can get!
